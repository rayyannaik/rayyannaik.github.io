Random Objects 3D Modelling

This project is a collection of small 3D models that I created for practice and experimentation. It includes fasteners, perfume and attar bottles, cap nuts, Allen screws, a hand gripper, and a simple mechanism.


About Project

Each object was modeled in AutoCAD with attention to proportion and shape. I tried to mix mechanical and everyday items to improve my technical accuracy and creative thinking. Some parts, like the fasteners and screws, helped me focus on precision, while others, like the bottles and gripper, allowed me to explore smooth curves and surface details.
