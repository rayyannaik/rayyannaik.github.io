Sheet Metal Design & Fabrication

This project showcases a series of sheet metal components designed for manufacturability. It features complex enclosures, fan guards, and mounting brackets, highlighting my ability to work with bend allowances, k-factors, and flat pattern exports.


About Project

Each component was modeled in SolidWorks using professional sheet metal tools. I focused on creating precise 3D geometries that can be seamlessly flattened for laser cutting and CNC folding.
